# New Creatures Plan - Phone Pet Paradise

## Overview
This document outlines all planned new creatures to expand the collection from 33 to 60+ characters while maintaining the kawaii chibi pixel art style.

---

## Character Categories

### 1. Hood Kids (Animal Hood Hoodies)
Cute kids wearing expressive animal hood hoodies with big eyes, rosy cheeks, and colorful designs.

### 2. Costume Kids (Full Body Costumes)
Kids in full-body themed costumes (food, objects, fantasy themes).

### 3. Chibi Animals
Adorable simplified animals with big heads, small bodies, and expressive faces.

### 4. Fantasy Creatures
Magical beings, spirits, and mythical creatures in kawaii style.

### 5. Food/Object Characters
Cute anthropomorphic food and objects with faces.

## DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Color Palettes by Biome
- **Meadow**: Greens, yellows, soft pinks, sky blues
- **Sunset**: Oranges, golds, warm pinks, purples
- **Night**: Deep purples, blues, silver, starlight white
- **Forest**: Earthy browns, deep greens, autumn oranges
- **Snow**: White, ice blue, soft pink, silver
- **City**: Bright primaries, neon accents, grays
- **Ruins**: Stone grays, gold, royal purple, dragon greens
- **Ocean**: Teals, aquas, coral pinks, deep blues
- **Space**: Deep purple, cosmic blues, star yellows, nebula pinks

### Character Style Requirements
1. **Size**: 64x64 pixels per frame
2. **Head-to-body ratio**: Large head (40-50% of height)
3. **Eyes**: Big, expressive, with sparkle highlights
4. **Cheeks**: Rosy/blushing accent
5. **Outlines**: Clean pixel art outlines
6. **Animation**: Bouncy, lively movement
7. **Expression**: Happy, friendly, welcoming

### Hood Kid Specific
- Hood covers most of head
- Animal face on hood top (eyes, ears, nose)
- Visible hair peeking from hood
- Simple shirt/shorts visible
- Hood can have tail attached

### Costume Kid Specific
- Full-body costume coverage
- Face visible through opening
- Costume-specific accessories
- Themed colors matching costume
